## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="circle-info" title="What is Tupley?">
    Tupley is an AI-powered no-code app builder that lets you create web apps, dashboards, and internal tools by simply describing your ideas in plain language.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="hammer" title="What can I build with Tupley?">
    Tupley can generate a wide variety of apps â€” from internal tools like CRMs, inventory trackers, and dashboards to lightweight customer-facing portals.  
    If you can describe it clearly in plain language, Tupley can usually create a working version of it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="plus" title="How do I create a new project?">
    Start at [Tupley](https://tupley.ai/), and type your idea into the prompt. You can also choose from preset templates like CRM, Inventory, or Project Management.  

    <Tip>
    Here is a quickstart guide for your very first project: [Quickstart](/quickstart)
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="graduation-cap" title="Do I need coding experience?">
    No. Tupley is designed for non-technical users.  
    All you need to do is describe what you want, and Tupley will generate it for you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="lightbulb" title="Any tips for effective prompts?">
    - Be specific about what you want the app to do  
    - Mention key features or workflows you expect  
    - Start simple, then refine your request step by step
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Building & Editing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="database" title="Which databases are supported?">
    Currently, only **Airtable** is supported.  

    <Warning>
    Integrations for Google Sheets, Notion, HubSpot, and Salesforce are **coming soon**.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="clock" title="How long does it take to generate an app?">
    Usually just a few minutes. Tupley processes your request step by step, prioritizes tasks, and shows progress in real time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="pen-to-square" title="Can I edit the app after it's generated?">
    Yes! You can refine and adjust fields, layouts, and features directly within Tupley by simply telling it what changes you'd like.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="download" title="Can I export my code?">
    <Warning>
    At this time, Tupley does not support exporting full source code.  
    The focus is on enabling rapid no-code building and deployment directly inside the platform.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="mobile" title="Can I build mobile apps with Tupley?">
    Tupley apps are web-based but fully responsive, so they work seamlessly on mobile devices.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="plug" title="Can I connect my app to APIs or third-party services?">
    <Warning>
    Not yet. At this time, Tupley supports direct integrations with specific databases (e.g. Airtable).  
    General API connections and webhook support are planned for future releases.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Deployment

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="rocket" title="How do I deploy my app?">
    Once generated, you can **preview** your app or **deploy & share** it with others via a link.  

    <Tip>
    A detailed guide is available here: [How to deploy](/deploy)
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="palette" title="Can I remove Tupley branding?">
    <Warning>
    Branding removal is not available at this time.
    </Warning>
    All apps display "Built with Tupley".
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="globe" title="Can I use Tupley with a custom domain?">
    Custom domain support is available on paid plans.  
    Free plans are limited to Tupley-hosted links.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Performance & Reliability

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="users" title="How many users can my Tupley app handle?">
    Tupley apps are designed to be lightweight and performant for small to medium-scale usage.  
    For production-scale or enterprise scenarios, please contact our team to discuss performance requirements and scaling options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="server" title="What happens if Tupley goes down?">
    We monitor uptime closely and use cloud redundancy to minimize downtime.  
    For free users, support is provided via our help center.  
    For paid plans, we aim to provide faster support response and priority assistance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
